Boba Workshop


where creativity sparked

“Boba Workshop” actually something called “Boba Drops”—a fun, beginner-friendly web development workshop where participants build a simple website using HTML (and CSS), and in return, they get free boba (bubble tea) as a reward.


How to participate :


Join the workshop:

Typically offered during Starthack (or HackClub events), “Boba Drops” runs for about an hour in a guided, beginner-friendly session.

Build a Simple Website:

Use HTML + CSS—you need at least five distinct HTML/CSS features (e.g., headings, links, lists, styling, etc.), and at least some CSS
AI-generated content is not allowed—it's all about your personal creative effort

Submit Your Site:

Submit your project per the guidelines (often via a GitHub or submission platform).
Ensure it meets accessibility and effort criteria—e.g., no tiny fonts or hard-to-read colors.

Get reward:

Once approved, you get a small grant (typically around $5) to buy yourself boba—by digital or physical channel.
